Welcome to Sia Satellite

Your gateway to decentralized cloud storage.

Sia Satellite allows renting decentralized storage even without owning crypto-currency. You can pay with your credit card and enjoy the benefits of using decentralized storage - privacy, reliability, and full control of your data.

However, at this point you are still required to use a specialized renting software like renterd. This means that you need to download it, install, and wait until it syncs with the blockchain before you can actually rent storage. You can read how to do it here.
